What is RBC?

ROBLOX Business Convention 2018 is set to be bigger and better than any event on ROBLOX before. We urge ROBLOX companies and services to head down to help promote their buisness and to get some new customers.

How much is it?

Entry to the event is free as well as sub-events within. Stalls may have items that you are able to buy. To get a stall, please join our discord and say '!stall' in the bot command channel, there is a small fee for better placement.

What percentage cost do you take from stalls?

Although we take a small fee, anything bought at the event will have the 30% ROBLOX fee as well as a 10% fee going to RBC18 staff, helping to pay for the event. Stalls can offer their profit to RBC18 instead of paying for placement although it is optional.
